The Sea of Glass

In the book of Revelation, the sea of glass represents the perfect peace and purity that exists before God's throne. It symbolizes the crystal-clear nature of divine truth and the serene worship of heaven. This sacred space speaks of perfect clarity. The sea of glass reminds us of the eternal rest and worship that awaits those who persevere in faith.
