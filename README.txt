Sea of Glass - Before the Throne
Inspired by Revelation 4:6
"And before the throne there was a sea of glass like unto crystal"


A PLACE OF PEACE AND REFLECTION

This website is inspired by the biblical vision of the sea of glass described in the book of Revelation. 
The sea of glass represents the perfect peace, purity, and holiness that exists before God's throne in heaven. 
This crystal-clear expanse symbolizes the divine separation between the earthly and heavenly realms, where 
the victorious saints stand in eternal worship and rest.

BIBLICAL SIGNIFICANCE:
- Represents God's holiness and purity (crystal clear, without blemish)
- Symbolizes peace and tranquility in God's presence
- A place where the victorious saints stand after overcoming trials
- Reflects God's glory like a perfect mirror
- Demonstrates the separation between the sacred and profane
- Represents the eternal rest awaiting faithful believers

DESIGN PHILOSOPHY:
- Clean, minimalist design reflecting the purity of divine truth
- Simple solid black background representing the void before creation
- White text symbolizing the light of God's word piercing the darkness
- Distraction-free layout focusing attention on the spiritual message
- Ready for customization with background imagery when desired
- Emphasizes content over visual effects to honor the sacred subject matter

BIBLICAL REFERENCES:

Revelation 4:6 (KJV):
"And before the throne there was a sea of glass like unto crystal: and in the midst of the throne, 
and round about the throne, were four beasts full of eyes before and behind."

Revelation 15:2 (KJV):
"And I saw as it were a sea of glass mingled with fire: and them that had gotten the victory over 
the beast, and over his image, and over his mark, and over the number of his name, stand on the 
sea of glass, having the harps of God."

The sea of glass appears in these two key passages in Revelation, representing different aspects 
of heavenly worship and victory over evil.


SPIRITUAL MEANING:

The sea of glass serves as a powerful symbol of:

1. PURITY: The crystal-clear nature represents the absolute holiness required to stand before God
2. PEACE: The calm, still waters symbolize the perfect rest found in God's presence
3. VICTORY: Those who overcome stand upon this sea, representing triumph over worldly trials
4. WORSHIP: The harps of God mentioned in Revelation 15:2 indicate eternal praise and worship
5. REFLECTION: Like a mirror, it reflects God's glory and the transformed nature of believers
6. SEPARATION: It creates a boundary between the holy throne room and all that is impure
